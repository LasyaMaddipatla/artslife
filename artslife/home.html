Art is a creative activity that expresses imaginative or technical skill.
It produces a product, an object. Art is a diverse range of human activities in creating visual,
performing artifacts, and expressing the author's imaginative mind.
Specific mediums and forms of artistic expression have changed throughout human history,
but for the most part, art falls into one of the following seven classical forms.
Each different form of art is experienced differently and affects our emotions and feelings.

Sculpture
Literature
